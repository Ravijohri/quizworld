RRB NTPC 2022 exam is to be conducted to recruit candidates for the posts of Junior Clerk cum Typist, Accounts Clerk cum Typist, Junior Time Keeper, Trains Clerk, Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Traffic Assistant, Goods Guard, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Account Assistant cum Typist, Senior Time Keeper, Commercial Apprentice and Station Master in various Zonal Railways and Production Units of Indian Railways.

"What is RRB NTPC Exam?"

  • The NTPC full form is Non-Technical Popular Categories. The RRB NTPC exam is conducted by the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) to recruit candidates for various posts in the non-technical categories in the Indian Railways. The candidates who have completed their graduation or have passed class XII can apply for the RRB NTPC posts.
  • The RRB NTPC 2022 exam is conducted in four stages – CBT 1, CBT 2, Typing Skill Test/Computer Based Aptitude Test (as applicable) and Document verification. The syllabus of the RRB NTPC exam is of Class 10, 12, and graduate levels and the exam is conducted in 15 languages.

    • "RRB NTPC Question Paper 2022: Candidates must solve the previous year’s RRB NTPC Question Paper to speed up their preparations for the Computer-based Aptitude Test (CBAT) 2022 exam. The RRB NTPC Previous Year Question Paper assists in a clearer understanding of the pattern of exam questions and enables candidates to identify their strongest and weakest topics. The RRB NTPC CBAT Exam for Pay Level 4 & 6 will be conducted on July 30, 2022."
